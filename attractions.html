Attractions

Discover the unforgettable natural and cultural highlights of Taniti.

Volcano Overlook Hike

Venture on a guided trek to the active volcano's peak for awe-inspiring natural views. This half-day excursion includes local storytelling, offering panoramic scenery perfect for photography. An essential adventure for energetic visitors.

Artisan Market & Local Crafts

Wander through the vibrant artisan market, browsing unique goods like handwoven baskets, shell jewelry, and carved wood statues. It's the perfect destination to find distinctive souvenirs and experience the island's dynamic artistic culture.

Helicopter Island Tour

Take to the air for an exhilarating helicopter trip around Taniti. See the dramatic contrast between lush rainforests, white sands, and rugged cliffs from above, including a spectacular mid-air view of the volcano's rim.

Make Reservation

Botanical Garden Walk

Explore the peaceful botanical gardens featuring hundreds of local plant species, vibrant flowers, and rare orchids. Guided tours share insights into the island's rich ecology and ongoing conservation efforts.

Beautiful Beaches

Relax on sun-kissed shores where crystalline waters meet soft white sand. Whether you prefer lively beach clubs or secluded coves, the island is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, shelling, or simply unwinding beneath a shady palm tree with a cold beverage.

Tastes of Taniti: Culinary Experience

Participate in an interactive dining event where local chefs demonstrate and prepare authentic island dishes using fresh ingredients. This hands-on session includes tastings and stories about culinary traditions passed down through generations.
